\subsection{Detailed Description}
Support and F\-A\-Q\-: visit \href{http://www.atmel.com/design-support/}{\tt Atmel Support}

This group provides a good handful of macros intended to smooth out the differences between various assemblers, similar to what \hyperlink{compiler_8h}{compiler.\-h} does for compilers, except that assemblers tend to be much less standardized than compilers.

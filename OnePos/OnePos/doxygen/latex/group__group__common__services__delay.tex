\subsection{Detailed Description}
Support and F\-A\-Q\-: visit \href{http://www.atmel.com/design-support/}{\tt Atmel Support}

This module provides simple loop-\/based delay routines for those applications requiring a brief wait during execution. Common A\-P\-I for U\-C3, X\-M\-E\-G\-A, and A\-V\-R M\-E\-G\-A.
